Danielle's Profile

My specialty is dealing with monsters that you can find in the water. These sea monsters can be exceptionally hard to deal with because the source of their special abilities are usually the tireless waves flowing in these lakes, rivers, and oceans. Although, after six years of monster hunting I’m getting the hang of these difficult water creatures. During my years at Monster Hunters Ltd, I have been fortunate enough to work in different fields from dragons to forest creatures to the ones that hide under your bed - all across the globe. As I continue to work as the newest sea monster intern, I hope to use these past experiences to tackle these challenging creatures. So, if you need help identifying a monster that you've never seen, I'm your gal! I've seen it all. Just request me in your message on our contact page.
